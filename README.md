## 📖 About The Project

MindSpace is a modern student learning platform designed to provide students with a personalized ecosystem for learning, productivity, and career development.

The goal of MindSpace is to bring together everything a student needs in one platform—from tracking daily habits and study sessions to following a personalized career roadmap and accessing curated learning resources.

Rather than switching between multiple apps, students can manage their complete learning journey inside MindSpace.
